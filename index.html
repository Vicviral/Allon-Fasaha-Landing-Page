Allon-Fasaha

Allon Fasaha is for Out-of-School Children (Almajiri) who do not have access to formal Education and lose out on future opportunities. The Allon Fasaha platform is a mobile learning platform that provides accelerated learning and integrates the “Almajiri” back into the formal education system.
